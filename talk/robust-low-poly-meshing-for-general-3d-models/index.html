Robust Low-Poly Meshing for General 3D Models

Zhen Chen, Zherong Pan, Kui Wu, Etienne Vouga, Xifeng Gao

Abstract

We propose a robust re-meshing approach that can automatically generate visual-preserving low-poly meshes for any high-poly models found in the wild. Our method can be seamlessly integrated into current mesh-based 3D asset production pipelines. Compared to state-of-the-art approaches and widely used software, our method demonstrates its superiority in terms of the element count, visual preservation, geometry and topology guarantees of the generated low-polys.
